Requirements

To use VoiceOver in Blio, system 4.0 or higher is required, and system 5.0 or higher is recommended for best experience. System 5.0 supports three-finger swipe, continuous reading, and navigation by line.

Reading

To open a book in your library, double tap on it.

To read, tap the center of the screen for the Book Page control and double tap to start reading.

To read through the book, swipe down with two fingers. By default, reading will then be continuous, until you stop by double tapping.

To stop reading and restore controls, double tap the center of the screen.

To close the book and go back to your library, double tap the “Library Back” button in the upper left corner of the screen.

Navigation

Set the rotor and flick with one finger up or down in an open book to move forward or back by character, word, or line.

Flick with three fingers right to advance to the next page or left to go back a page.

Jump to any page with the Page Chooser control in the top right corner of the screen of an open book or double tap the table of contents button to display chapters.

Text-to-Speech Voices

Besides reading books with VoiceOver, Blio supports reading with high quality text-to-speech voices. You can download a voice here.
