Requirements

To use VoiceOver in Book Place, iOS 4.0 or higher is required. iOS 5.0 or higher is recommended for best experience. With iOS 5.0, Book Place supports three-finger swipe, continuous reading, and navigation by line.

Reading

To open a book in your library, double tap on the title.

To read, tap the center of the screen so that "Book Page" is spoken. Double tap to start reading.

To read through the book, swipe down with two fingers. By default, reading will then be continuous. You can pause by tapping the screen with two fingers.

To stop reading and restore controls, double tap the center of the screen.

To close the book and go back to your library, double tap the Library Back button in the upper left corner of the screen.

Navigation

Set the rotor and flick with one finger up or down in an open book to move forward or back by character, word, or line.

Flick with three fingers left to advance to the next page or right to go back a page.

Jump to any page with the Page Chooser control, which is near the top of the screen when a book is open and controls are shown. To display chapters, double tap the Table of Contents button.

Text-to-Speech Voices

Besides reading books with VoiceOver, Book Place supports reading with high quality text-to-speech voices. You can download a voice here.
