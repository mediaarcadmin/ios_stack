Fixed View Navigation Tips

To advance to the next page in Fixed view, swipe your finger right to left on the screen; to go back to the previous page, swipe from left to right. To show or hide the top and bottom toolbars, tap on the center of the screen.

To navigate through the book, tap the pie-chart icon at the right of the top toolbar. This will bring down a control whose button you can slide to move to the desired point of the book. The same control is available in Flowed view.

To zoom in to a set level, double-tap on the screen. To zoom in however much you want, touch thumb and forefinger to the screen and spread them apart. Once zoomed, you can pan around the page and to the next page by moving your finger on the screen.

To zoom to a block of text, double-tap on the block. To go to the next block of text, tap once on the right third of the screen. To go to the previous block, tap on the left third.
